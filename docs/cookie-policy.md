# COOKIE AND TRACKING POLICY: ZERO-TRACKING ARCHITECTURE
**Entity:** Information Security Consulting SRL  
**Standard:** GDPR ePrivacy Directive, VDA ISA 6.0  
**Status:** Released - Mandatory Minimalist  

---

## 1. OUR SECURITY STANCE ON TRACKING
At itis-secure, we treat "Convenience Tracking" as a potential vulnerability. Our portal is architected to prioritize user privacy and data sovereignty over marketing analytics.

**We do not use:**
- Advertising or third-party tracking cookies.
- Cross-site behavior trackers.
- Intrusive analytic platforms (Google Analytics, etc.).

---

## 2. STRICTLY NECESSARY COOKIES
The only cookies allowed within our "Digital Fortress" are those strictly necessary for the technical operation of the portal (Art. 5(3) ePrivacy Directive).

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Retention |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| `__secure_session` | Maintains session integrity and prevents CSRF attacks. | Session |
| `compliance_ack` | Records your acknowledgment of our security standards. | 365 Days |

---

## 3. TECHNICAL LOGS (AUDIT TRAIL)
In accordance with ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Control A.8.15 (Logging), our servers record standard technical telemetry:
- IP Address (Anonymized).
- Browser Agent Strings.
- Request Timestamps.

These logs are kept for a maximum of 30 days for security forensic purposes only and are never cross-referenced for profiling.

---

## 4. YOUR SOVEREIGNTY
As we do not use non-essential cookies, no consent banner is required by Law. However, you can manage and delete these technical cookies directly via your browser's security settings.
