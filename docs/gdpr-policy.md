## 1. TRANSPARENCY AND DATA SOVEREIGNTY
In accordance with **Articles 13 and 14 of the GDPR**, Information Security Consulting SRL (itis-secure) provides this transparency declaration for all data subjects engaging with our technical portals. 

We operate under the principle of **Data Minimization (Art. 5(1)(c))**, ensuring that only the technical data required for mission-critical consulting is processed.

---

## 2. DATA PROCESSING PROTOCOLS (CONTACT FORM)
When you submit an inquiry through our "Audit Readiness" terminal, the following data is processed:
- **Identity Data:** Name and Company affiliation.
- **Contact Data:** Email address for audit-related correspondence.
- **Contextual Data:** Service interest (TISAX, ISO 27001, etc.).

**Legal Basis:** 
- **Art. 6(1)(b):** Necessary for pre-contractual steps at the request of the data subject.
- **Art. 6(1)(f):** Legitimate interest in maintaining secure and traceable communications for security assessments.

---

## 3. TECHNICAL DEPLOYMENT AND SECURITY (TOMs)
Our "Digital Fortress" implementation includes the following Technical and Organizational Measures (TOMs), ensuring compliance with **Art. 32 GDPR**:
- **Protocol Enforcement:** Mandatory TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit.
- **Integrity Protection:** HSTS with preload to prevent downgrade and Man-in-the-Middle (MITM) attacks.
- **Non-Repudiation:** Audited session logs and secure storage pipelines.
- **Zero-Tracking:** Absence of behavioral or profiling cookies.

---

## 4. DATA SUBJECT RIGHTS ENFORCEMENT
Data subjects may exercise their rights by contacting our Data Protection Officer directly. We prioritize these requests as "Critical Incidents":
1. **Right to Access (Art. 15):** Request a full transcript of your data profile.
2. **Right to Erasure (Art. 17):** Secure deletion of inquiries once consulting is concluded.
3. **Right to Portability (Art. 20):** Provision of your compliance roadmap data in structured format.

---

## 5. SUB-PROCESSORS AND AUDIT TRAILS
itis-secure utilizes only hardened, ISO 27001 certified sub-processors for portal hosting and telemetry. Data remains within the European Economic Area (EEA) or in jurisdictions with an Equivalency Decision.
